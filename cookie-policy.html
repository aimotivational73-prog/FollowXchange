1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other devices when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve the user experience, and provide analytical and advertising information to the owners of the site.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, but your access to some functionality and areas (such as earning points via ads) may be severely restricted or broken.
