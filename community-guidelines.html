Built with love for creators

1 Be Respectful 🤝

We are a community of creators helping each other grow. Treat all community members with kindness and respect.

  • No harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior toward other users.
  • Absolutely no hate speech based on religion, gender, ethnicity, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic.
  • Keep all communications friendly and constructive.

2 Be Honest 💯

Honesty is the core of our exchange system. If you want real followers, you must provide real follows.

  • Only submit real proof: When completing a follow task, only upload genuine screenshots of your action.
  • No fake screenshots: Submitting photoshopped, downloaded, or reused screenshots is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not claim points for tasks or actions that you did not actually complete.

3 Follow The Rules ✅

When you accept a task to follow someone, you are making a commitment to that user.

  • If you accept a follow task, you must follow through and complete it accurately.
  • The 7-Day Rule: You must remain following the account for a minimum of 7 days.
  • Do not follow a user to claim your points and then immediately unfollow them. This is known as "Follow/Unfollow abuse" and our system tracks it!

4 One Account Rule 👤

To keep the platform fair and prevent abuse of our points and referral systems, we enforce a strict account limit.

  • Each person is allowed only ONE account.
  • Creating multiple or "burner" accounts to abuse the welcome bonus or farm points will result in a permanent ban of all associated accounts.
  • Exception: Family members living in the same household may have separate accounts, provided they use different devices, different email addresses, and different Instagram accounts.

5 No Automation 🤖

FollowXchange is a platform for real people. Automated actions ruin the experience for everyone.

  • Do not use bots, scripts, or macros to complete follow tasks.
  • Do not use automation tools or auto-clickers to watch ads.
  • Do not use VPNs or proxy servers to manipulate your location or abuse the system.

6 Account Safety 🔒

Your security is your responsibility. Keep your account safe!

  • Never share your FollowXchange password with anyone.
  • We will NEVER ask for your Instagram password. Ever.
  • If you receive suspicious messages on Telegram or email claiming to be from FollowXchange asking for passwords or money, report them to our official admin immediately.

7 Advertising Conduct 📺

Ads are how we keep FollowXchange free for everyone. Please respect the advertising ecosystem.

  • Watch video and display ads genuinely.
  • If you ever have your own ads placed on the network, do not attempt to click your own ads.
  • Do not use ad-blockers while using FollowXchange. Ad-blockers prevent you from earning points and defeat the purpose of the free platform.

8 Consequences for Violations ⚖️

We actively monitor the platform. If you break these guidelines, we will enforce the following consequences depending on the severity of the action:

Warning

Issued for first-time minor offenses, such as uploading a blurry screenshot by mistake.

Points Deduction

Issued for repeated minor offenses or early unfollowing. We will deduct the points you wrongly earned.

Temporary Suspension

Your account will be locked for a set period for major offenses, like consistently breaking the 7-day rule.

Permanent Ban

Immediate removal for severe violations: fraud, using multiple accounts, fake proofs, or extreme harassment.

9 Reporting Bad Behavior 🚩

We rely on our community to help keep the platform clean.

  • If you spot a user using fake proofs, harassing others, or breaking rules, report them immediately to our admin via Telegram: @[YOUR_TELEGRAM].
  • Please provide the offending user's FollowXchange Username or Instagram Handle, along with a brief description or screenshot of the issue.
  • We take all reports seriously and review them confidentially.

10 Positive Community 🌟

FollowXchange is more than just a tool; it's a network of real creators!

  • Help new users understand how to use the platform if they ask for advice.
  • Provide genuine engagement on Instagram! If you like the content of the person you are following, leave a like or a comment.
  • Together, we can build the most trustworthy and effective follow exchange community on the internet!
